The federal contracting world wasn't built for small businesses with no back office.

Filter 1
Look at contract solicitations only
On SAM.gov, focus on actual contract opportunities. Do not mix them up with grants and cooperative agreements unless that is intentionally your lane.
Filter 2
Set a practical value range
Use an award value range that fits your current capacity. Bigger is not always better. Bigger can also mean "you are walking into a knife fight wearing a cardigan."
Filter 3
Layer in NAICS + set-aside fit
Use the codes and designations you actually hold. Relevance beats volume. Ten qualified opportunities beat a hundred random ones every day of the week.
